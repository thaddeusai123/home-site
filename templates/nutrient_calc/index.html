Mixing Order

  1. Fill container with water.
  2. Add Masterblend, stir until dissolved.
  3. Add Epsom Salt, stir until dissolved.
  4. Add Calcium Nitrate last, stir until dissolved.
  5. Check pH (target 5.5–6.5), adjust with pH down if needed.

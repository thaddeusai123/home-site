⚡ GPIO Lab

Probe your Raspberry Pi's GPIO pins to figure out which ones fire each relay. Most relay HATs are active-low — a brief LOW pulse on the right pin clicks the relay. Some boards are active-high. Try LOW first; if nothing clicks, switch to HIGH.
